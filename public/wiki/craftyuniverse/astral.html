Astral

"KIIIIIIIIIING ASTRAL SPOREM DECOMPLE MYCELI SHROOMEL MOLDUM FUNGULUS IV, AT YOUR SERVICE!"

Astral is a mushroom person, a biological wonder, as they effectively are mushrooms that can walk and talk, but have no relations to mushrooms in the slightest. He's taken over a forest called "The Starlit Forest" all for himself (and his 20,000 children). Usually just known as "that annoying guy from the forest."

Trivia

  • He's a femboy, (though he won't admit it)
  • He's been married 3 times, two ended up in his partner dying and one ended up divorcing him.
  • Surprisingly good father at times
