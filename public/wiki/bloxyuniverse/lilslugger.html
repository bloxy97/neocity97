Li'l Slugger

"come down today

buy blox-flakes

and then we will

release santa"

Li'l Slugger is a cereal mascot for a defunct brand that quickly went under because someone sneaked plutonium into the recipe. Her cries of "BUY MY CEREAL" ring through the desolate landscape as she has no home.
