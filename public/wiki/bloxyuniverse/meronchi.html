メロンチー lit. Dear Melon (Meron-chi)

"Hm? This watermelon looks weird... It's goober Meron-chi. You aren't water!!!"

メロンチー (Mostly referred as Meron-chi) is a Melon creature found on continent SandLand. He's incredibly strong but extremely lazy at the same time. His favorite activity is doing nothing. He only speaks Japanese.

Trivia

  • He knows CoCo has a crush on him, but it doesnt really go anywhere because of his language barrier.
  • メロンチー suffers from Olimar Syndrome.
  • His mother got eaten by a elephant as a child.
