CoCo

"Hm? There's something hard in my ice cream... It's goober CoCo. Get out of my ice cream!!!"

CoCo is a coconut-like creature found on continent SandLand. She is almost completely unaware of her surroundings and mostly spends her time in sand. her crush is Meron-chi, but she has no idea what a crush is.

Trivia

  • CoCo is based off a fake coconut from a Suika Game recreation.
  • Everyone pretends to like her but none of them actually do.
  • CoCo has no friends.
