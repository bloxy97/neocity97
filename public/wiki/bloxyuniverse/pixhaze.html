ピク霧 lit. Pixel Fog (Pixhaze)

"Why is my camera so fuzzy... It's goober Pixhaze. You look weird."

Pixhaze is a thing that we arent even sure is real. It hangs out in rain and snow and likes to make weird noises with its eyes.

Trivia
