Suspicious Muffins

"...What muffins? i never ate a muffin today."

Suspicious Muffins are just normal muffins but they have weird and freaky effects on the people around it. The owner is very forgetful about where they put the muffins but only remembers when the muffins are stolen. It makes everyone around it want to take the muffins.
